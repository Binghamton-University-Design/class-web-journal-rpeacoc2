Project 1: Riddle Me This

This project was a collaberation of the whole class to produce a book of riddles that when solved are movie titles. We began this back at the beginning of the semester in seperate groups. We then came together as a class to create the topic of movie titles. From here, we snowballed a bunch of ideas and finally came up, with much deliberation, with our final book.

Project 3: Digital Storytelling

For this project, I've chosen to tell a murder mystery story, inspired by clue. In this game, you are invited to a dinner at the Mayor's house along with two other guests and his two servants. During the dinner, you learn a little more about the mayor and why each person was invited. Suddenly, the lights go out, a scream is heard, and when the lights go on you discover the Mayor has been killed! Play the game to ask questions to each person to find out who and why someone would kill the beloved mayor (This game is for entertainment and not educational).
